Learning for Life Programme (LLP)
=================================

The school’s Learning for Life Programme (LLP), **Game for Life** is aimed at encouraging students’ participation in a wide range of outdoor and sporting activities. to help them discover for themselves an activity (or activities) that they can pursue for a lifetime, in order to maintain  an active and healthy lifestyle.

Olympic values of respect, friendship and excellence are inculcated and reinforced through teachable moments as students engage in the activities.
