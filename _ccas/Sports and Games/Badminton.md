### About us

The TWSS Badminton Club aims to develop passion, discipline and teamwork among the players. Through regular training sessions, the students learn to improve their badminton skills, develop leadership qualities and build good relationships.

As part of their development within the CCA, the players also participate in friendly games and competitions.
