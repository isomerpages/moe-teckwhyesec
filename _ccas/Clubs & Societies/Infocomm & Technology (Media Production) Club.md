### About us

The aim of the club is to equip students with the skills and knowledge in the following areas :

#### Digital Photography and Videography Division

*   Create meaningful montages and videos to commemorate major school and national events.
*   Provide a platform for students to participate in national photography and videography competitions.

#### AVA 

*   Provide effective AVA services to the school during assemblies and major school events.

#### ICT 

*   Provide a platform for students to participate in ICT-related competitions  
    and visits to ICT-related industries.
*   Provide opportunities for students to learn about software to develop mobile applications.
*   To raise awareness on current issues, environmental conservation and National Education commemorative events through digital displays.
